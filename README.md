# FlowPay (FPAY) — Supply Model

## Overview

This document explains how FPAY token supply is structured between circulating and locked tokens.

Understanding supply distribution is critical for price stability and long-term sustainability.

---

## Supply Categories

1. Circulating Supply
Tokens actively available in the market.

2. Locked Supply
Tokens reserved for:
- team
- ecosystem
- long-term incentives

---

## Design Logic

- Too much circulating supply → high sell pressure  
- Too little circulating supply → low liquidity  

Balance is key.

---

## Example Structure

- 40% circulating  
- 60% locked (vesting over time)

---

## Key Insight

Token supply is not just numbers.

It directly affects:
- price behavior  
- investor confidence  
- long-term growth
